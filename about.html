Rock, paper and scissors

Rock, paper and scissors, also called in some regions of Brazil jokenpô[1][2] (from the Japanese じゃんけんぽん, jankenpon), is a recreational and simple hand game for two or more people, which requires no equipment or skill .

The game is often used as a selection method (as in choosing teams for sports, for example), as well as throwing coins, throwing dice, among others. However, unlike these methods that rely solely on luck, rock-paper-scissors can be played with a little skill. Especially if the game lasts for several turns with the same player, he or she can recognize and explore the logic of the opponent's behavior (perceive and anticipate the opponent's moves).
